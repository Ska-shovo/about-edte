About EdTE

Definition

Educational Technology and Engineering (EdTE) is an interdisciplinary field that focuses on designing, developing, implementing, and evaluating technological tools and systems to enhance teaching and learning processes.

Curriculum

Offered Courses: Instructional Design, Learning Analytics, AI in Education, AR/VR for Learning, Human-Computer Interaction, Software Development for Education.

Lab Activities: Developing educational apps, creating virtual reality simulations, analyzing learning data sets, building interactive learning modules.

Differences from Traditional Subjects

Unlike traditional subjects that focus on a single domain, EdTE is inherently interdisciplinary, blending pedagogy, psychology, computer science, and design thinking. It emphasizes practical application and creation over theoretical knowledge alone.

Importance in the Modern Era

EdTE is crucial for personalizing education, bridging geographical gaps, and preparing students for a tech-driven workforce. It provides practical skills in both educational theory and software engineering.

History of EdTE

Global Progress

The history of EdTE traces back to the early 20th century with teaching machines, evolving through the computer-aided instruction of the 80s, the rise of the internet and LMS in the 90s, to the current era of AI, mobile learning, and immersive technologies.

Progress in Bangladesh

In Bangladesh, EdTE has gained significant momentum with governmental initiatives like 'Digital Bangladesh'. The adoption of online learning platforms, digital content development, and blended learning models in universities marks a significant step forward.

Future Demands

The demand for EdTE professionals is projected to grow rapidly. Key future areas include personalized learning paths powered by AI, adaptive assessment systems, corporate training and reskilling platforms, and the ethical implementation of technology in education. Skills in data science, AI, and immersive technologies will be particularly valuable.

Success Story of EDTE

Salman Amin Khan

University: Massachusetts Institute of Technology (SB, MEng), Harvard University (MBA)

Success Story: Salman Khan founded "Khan Academy" with the vision of providing free, world-class education to anyone, anywhere. Starting with simple YouTube videos, his platform now serves millions of learners globally.

Contribution: He revolutionized online education by creating a vast library of interactive video lessons and practice exercises, making quality education accessible and personalized through technology.

Beverly Park Woolf

University: University of Massachusetts Amherst

Success Story: Beverly Park Woolf has advanced the use of artificial intelligence in education through her pioneering research. Her Intelligent Tutoring Systems (ITS) have helped thousands of students worldwide receive personalized learning experiences.

Contribution: She has designed innovative teaching methods by applying AI and machine learning to analyze student learning behaviors and emotions, opening new horizons in educational technology.

Punya Mishra

University: Arizona State University, USA

Success Story: Punya Mishra is a global leader in educational technology, known for his work on creativity and innovation in learning. He has published 200+ articles, earned $11M+ in research grants, and ranks among the top 2% of scientists worldwide.

Contribution: Punya Mishra developed the widely-used TPACK framework for integrating technology in education. He co-hosts the Silver Lining for Learning webinar series and led the award-winning Master of Arts in Educational Technology program at Michigan State University.

Ayman Sadiq

University: Institute of Business Administration, University of Dhaka

Success Story: Founded 10 Minute School in 2015, which is now the largest online education platform in Bangladesh with over 9 million students.

Contribution: Revolutionized access to education by offering free video lessons, live classes, quizzes, and model tests for students from primary to university levels.

Shahir Chowdhury

University: Institute of Business Administration, University of Dhaka

Success Story: Founder and CEO, Shikho, a rapidly growing edtech startup in Bangladesh. He was listed in Bloomberg New Economy Catalysts 2023 for his contributions.

Contribution: Introduced gamified, Bengali-language online learning content that focuses on deep student engagement, test preparation, and career-focused skill development.

Rahim Hossain

Success Story: Rahim, an EDTE alum, founded a startup that provides e-learning solutions across Asia.

Contribution: Guest lectures at EDTE and funds alumni networking events.

Q: Do I need to be a programmer to study EdTE?

A: While programming skills are highly beneficial, they are not always a strict prerequisite. The field welcomes people from diverse backgrounds, including education, psychology, and design. However, a willingness to learn technical concepts is key.
